ABOUT US

Discover Youth For Rural Health

Youth for Rural Health, a student-led voluntary organization recently founded by a group of motivated students from the University of Dhaka, is committed to improving healthcare access in rural communities.

With mission is to improve health outcomes by addressing the essential needs of underserved areas through accessible healthcare services, health education, and focused initiatives on hygiene and menstrual health. By bridging gaps in rural healthcare access, we seek to create meaningful, lasting change.

We believe in the power of youth leadership to drive this transformation. Through our programs, young volunteers take active roles in community health, fostering a generation that values wellness, inclusivity, and resilience

OUR MISSION

Our Mission & Goals

To see every rural community empowered with accessible, quality healthcare and well-informed citizens who actively participate in sustaining healthy lifestyles. We envision a future where rural health disparities are minimized, primary healthcare is strengthened, and universal health coverage is achieved for all.
